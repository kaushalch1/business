Business Board Game - Help

Goal

Become the richest player by the end of the game.

Your final score is: current balance + property value - loan due.

Players and Turns

  • There are 4 players: Player 1, Player 2, Player 3, and Player 4.
  • Players take turns in order.
  • Click the dice button to play your turn.
  • If you roll 6, you get one extra turn.

Board Rules

  • When you pass GO, the bank gives you ₹10000 as a reward.
  • If you land on Rest House, you get a bonus of ₹11000.
  • If you land on Club, you must pay ₹4000.
  • If you land on Jail, you cannot move for the next 2 turns.
  • Tax and Wealth Tax squares make you lose money to the bank.
  • If you land on a property that nobody owns, you can buy it.
  • If another player owns the property, you may need to pay rent instead.

Buy and Rent

  • Use Buy when you want to purchase the property you land on.
  • If you do not buy, rent may be charged.
  • If the square belongs to another player, rent goes to that player.
  • If the square belongs to the bank, the bank receives the money.

Loans

  • Take Loan gives the active player ₹10000.
  • A player can only take a loan if they do not already have one.
  • Loan amount is shown in red text near the player balance, so you can quickly see how much money is owed.
  • The balance display is shown in green text, which means the money the player currently has.
  • Loan grows with interest over time.(For every 5 turns it grows by 10%)
  • Pay Loan clears the loan when the player has enough balance.

End of Game

  • The game ends when all purchasable cards are bought.
  • A popup appears with Player, Loan, Assets, Current Balance, and Total.
  • Players are sorted by total score.
  • The player with the highest total is the winner.

Quick Play

  1. Click the dice.
  2. Move according to the result.
  3. Choose Buy or Rent if shown.
  4. Manage loans carefully.
  5. Try to finish with the highest total score.
